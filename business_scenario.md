# Business Scenario Notes

## Project Purpose
Supporting investment, operations, and compliance reporting for portfolio and client management.

## Data Sources
- Multiple internal systems providing disparate data
- Investment data: portfolios, assets, market values
- Operations data: trades, settlements, transactions
- Compliance data: regulatory rules, compliance checks, pass/fail status

## Business Goals
- Create standardized, accurate reporting across all datasets
- Ensure data consistency and reliability
- Support global operating model with unified reporting standards
- Enable data-driven decision making for portfolio management

## Key Challenges
- Data comes from multiple source systems with different formats
- Need to consolidate and harmonize data
- Ensure accuracy and completeness for regulatory compliance
- Provide timely insights for investment decisions

## Success Criteria
- All data validated and reconciled
- Standardized reporting format implemented
- Dashboards and reports meet business requirements
- Data quality metrics tracked and improved
